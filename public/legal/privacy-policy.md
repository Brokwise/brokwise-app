However, please be aware that certain data may be retained by us even after you withdraw your consent, as some provisions will continue to apply beyond the termination of the agreement according to the terms of use of our Platform. 4. **Right to Erasure / Deletion:** You have the right to request deletion of your personal data where: (a) it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected; (b) you withdraw consent (where processing was based on consent); (c) you object to processing based on legitimate interests; or (d) the data has been unlawfully processed. This right is subject to our legal obligations to retain certain data for compliance purposes.

5. **Right to Access:** You have the right to access the personal information we store about you. To exercise this right, you may send us an email from your e-mail address. We will respond to your request within the reasonable timeframes and provide you with information about the personal data we hold.

6. **Right to nominate:** You may nominate any individual to exercise your rights under this Privacy Policy and applicable law in the event of your death or incapacity.
